About Us

Our Mission

Preeminent Library was founded with one goal in mind and that is to make meaningful books highly accessible. We focus on nonfiction and memoir, genres we believe have the most real impact on people's lives.

Hours

Monday – Friday: 9am to 7pm
Saturday: 10am to 4:30pm
Sunday: Closed

Location

We are located in downtown New Haven, Connecticut. Parking is available on the street and in the lot behind the building. Parking can also be found by the New Haven green.

Membership

Membership is free and open to all residents. Stop by the front desk with a valid ID to sign up. Members can borrow up to 4 books at a time for up to 3 weeks. Late fees apply.
