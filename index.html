Can't Hurt Me

A memoir about discipline, mental toughness, and pushing past the limits most people accept for themselves. His first book of a 2 part series.

Never Finished

A follow-up work to Can't Hurt Me centered on growth, never finished goals, and the idea that personal development is never really complete. One can always push further.

Confessions of an Economic Hit Man

A memoir depicting the story of John Perkins. Revealing a secret dark side to the United States filled with deception and secrecy. The first in his 2 part series.

Author: John Perkins

Authors

David Goggins

David Goggins is a former Navy SEAL, endurance athlete, and motivational speaker known for writing about resilience, discipline, self-mastery, and pushing beyond human limits.

John Perkins

John Perkins is an author and speaker best known for writing about international finance, power structures, and economic influence in global politics. He has faced high levels of criticism after publishing Confessions of an Economic Hit Man.
